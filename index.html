Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Turtle Dive does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. The app does not require an account, login, or any personal data to play.

Turtle Dive uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data such as device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data to serve relevant ads. This data is handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Turtle Dive does not collect data from children and is rated 4+ with no objectionable content.
